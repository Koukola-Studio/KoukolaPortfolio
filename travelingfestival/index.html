Brandýský Cestovatelský Festival

— Visual Identity

— The "Brandýský cestovatelský festival" is a social event where travellers share their experiences and knowledge from their visits to different countries. In total, there are 8 travel lectures by experienced travelers. Each lecture lasts 1 hour and 15 minutes. The event in total is spread over 3 days. The event is organized by the foundation "Klub SRDCAŘŮ" from Brandýs nad Labem. All proceeds from the event are donated to charity.

Preparation

— 01

— The target group for this event is all ages. Therefore, the characters chosen are both playful and formal. The Brandýský cestovatelský festival is a narration of the experiences and knowledge of a particular traveller. Therefore, the brand strategy also adds features that characterize travel and discovery.

— The visual identity should be both important and playful. That's why I decided to combine clean and elegant typography that will complement each other together with the illustrations.

Design of visual identity

— 02

— We are now getting to the main part of designing the visual identity. The visual identity consists of typography that informs about the name of the event(Brandýský cestovatelský festival). For the logotype, I chose a classic and clean font, which I stretched at the last word of the event(FESTIVAL) to define the sign of playfulness.

— I placed illustrations between the letters of the keyword. The illustrations consist of dolls, a boat, a backpack, and birds. Putting them together symbolizes people, travelers who are discovering their new knowledge and experiences e.g. a climber, a person who looks down on birds, and a person who travels on a boat.
