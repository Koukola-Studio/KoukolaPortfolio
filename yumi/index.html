Yumi

— Visual Identity for bistro

— Yumi is a bistro based on healthy food and fresh ingredients. Bistro Yumi is only in Rokycany, but they are trying to build a chain of bistros. Therefore, we had to create a logo that is simple and corporate.

Preparation

— 01

— The target group for Yumi Bistro is all ages. As it is a fresh bistro so the signs of freshness and healthy food are added.

— The green colour in the background is a symbol of leaf colouring because the bistro has lettuce leaves as the key ingredient.

Design of visual identity

— 02

— The logo consists of a short logo and a long logo.

— The short logo is formed by a circle and in it are leaves with a stem. The leaf is meant to represent freshness and a healthy diet. The leaf is in a position to be able to render each letter of the company name on the logo.

— The long logo was created by removing the circle from the short logo and inverting the fill. The leaf replaces the dot on the lowercase i.
